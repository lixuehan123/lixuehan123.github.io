Project Overview

The player switches between movement lanes to avoid incoming hazards and collects healing items to maintain their condition. When cannabis exposure lowers the status value past a threshold, movement controls reverse and familiar dodging actions become harder to manage. The player must continue avoiding danger, seek treatment, gradually restore their condition, and complete the sequence.

My Contributions

  • Design and Programming: I participated in the Game Jam as both a designer and programmer.
  • Scene Transitions: I implemented transitions and flow between scenes.
  • Character Actions: I created character actions and their supporting logic.
  • UI and Animation: I produced status information, interface feedback, and related animation.
  • Seamless Background: I implemented a seamless background for continuous movement.

Implementation Focus

Status-Driven Control Changes

Control reversal is not a separate gimmick; it is tied directly to the player's status value. Exposure to danger changes the control scheme, making status management affect movement difficulty.

Visual Continuity During Movement

Scene transitions, the seamless background, character actions, UI, and animation must remain synchronized so the player can read danger, current status, and recovery feedback while moving continuously.
