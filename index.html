About Me

Hello, I am Xuehan Li, a level designer with a background in architecture. I earned my undergraduate degree from Southeast University, where I developed a strong foundation in architectural thinking. I aim to integrate architectural theory into level design to create distinctive spatial experiences. I am currently pursuing a Master of Interactive Technology in Level Design at SMU Guildhall. My work combines visual cues, spatial circulation, player guidance, typology, gameplay mechanics, architectural structure, and atmosphere. I am also comfortable with UE5 Blueprints, scripting, and numerical analysis. Thank you for taking the time to explore my work.
